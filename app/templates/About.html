Answering Policy
  1. View answers to hundreds of questions:

    With IIITH Stack Overflow you can view hundreds of questions that the members of the community regularly post and answer. What's even better is that you don't need to sign up to view any of the answers.

  2. Ask a Question:

    With IIITH Stack Overflow you can ask questions on myraid subjects and be assured that you'll be answered. All you need to do is Register with the community and open doors to answers to all your questions!... Well almost ;)

  3. Answer a Question:

    Think you are the wise one? Well prove it! After registering with the community, you get to answer all you want and claim the bragging rights. What's even better is that with the reputation system you can be assured that your quality answers always reach the top and pehaps even be rated as the best possible answer.

  4. Bonus:

    Want more clarification to an answer? Want to clarify your question or somebosy else's answer? Registering with the community enables you to post comments.
